Beignets Recipes

  • Serves 4
  • About 10 mins to prepare

A confection made famous by Cafe du Monde in the French Market in New Orleans. Everytime I go out west everyone always asks me to make beignets, which I do not mind but it can be rather time consuming because of this I have a quick beignet recipe that everyone loves and is infact how we made ours growing up. It is very simple and can be fun for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of biscuit dough
  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar

Directions

  1. You will start by heating up your oil in a small pot.
  2. While your oil is heating open the biscuit dough and cut each biscuit in half.
  3. Once your oil has reached temp drop in about 3 or 4 biscuit halves at a time cook for about 30 seconds on one side then flip.
  4. Let the other side cook for an additional 20-30 seconds and remove.
  5. Add cooked biscuits to a bowl with a paper towel lining the inside.
  6. Transfer to another bowl, add powdered sugar and toss.
  7. Enjoy!
